Introduction 

OLG (OnlineLearningGround) is a MATLAB program with c++ mex files for demonstrating 
a non-holonomic ground vehicle motion computation up to the scale via ground predicction/classification. 
FOr details of the algorithm, please refer to the following delated publication:
